\section{Some more equations}\label{app:theory:more}
%
Signal summation for an array of $N$ receivers ideally leads to an increased signal-to-noise-ratio by $\sqrt{N}$,
\begin{equation*}
	\dfrac{\snr_{N}}{\snr_{n}}= \sqrt{N}.
\end{equation*}
